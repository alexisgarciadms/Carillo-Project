Above and Beyond Remodeling

As previously mentioned, there’s so much more to what we do at Carrillo Home Improvement in Dover, New Jersey than just remodeling. We tend to go above and beyond it all. One way we accomplish this is through our re-flooring services.

Covering it All

Whether you’ve had damage to your current floors or you’re just looking to change things up, we’re generally pretty adaptable to whatever you’re looking to do. We can work with hardwood floors, laminate, and even tile floors. Plus, at Carrillo Home Improvement, we only work with the highest quality products out on the market. That way you know, whenever you’re working with us, you’re ONLY getting the best of the best with both service and re-flooring. That’s our promise to you.

Ready to Get Started?

Ready to get things started? We’ve got you – and your floors – covered! Reach out to our team of experts for a free consultation today, and we’ll tell you all the steps in the process. We’ll also tell you what we’ll need from you, a general idea of how long it could take, and gladly answer any questions you might have. By the time we’re done, your home will be complete with some shiny new flooring!
