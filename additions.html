Adding to Your Home

Sometimes, the project is more than just re-envisioning your bathroom or kitchen or re-flooring your home. You may be in the market to add entire new rooms onto your home. Whether you feel like your family need some more space in your home or you have that extra room you’ve always wanted to add-on, Carrillo Home Improvement in Dover, NJ is ready to make it all happen.

Mapping it All Out

Obviously, there’s a lot of variables that come into play when we’re doing our initial consultation. We want to make sure that the dimensions are just right, and that it can be accomplished while fitting in seamlessly with the current structure. During this process, we’ll ask for lots of input from you, such as figuring out how much additional room you’re looking to have, the overall shape and layout you have in mind, and more. It all begins with the design process.

Additional Work

At Carrillo Home Improvement, we are experts in pretty much every aspect of the renovation and construction process. It comes with the territory of being around as long as we have. In addition to the standard construction of the room, we’ll also work with the trim, the overall framework, the installation of the windows and doors, painting, and more. Our focus is on both the interior as well as the exterior of your home, so no matter what you need, we can pretty much handle it all!
